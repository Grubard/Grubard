How SmartCart Works

Add Your Household

Once you add the members you'd like to share your SmartCart with, everyone will be able to view and update the same lists.

Fill Up Your Pantry

Add in everything that you want to keep track of. Items that you mark as a necessity will be automatically added to your grocery list when they fall below the desired quantity.

Go Shopping

When it's time to stock up, add anything else you need to your shopping list. Anything you check off the list will be automatically added to your pantry!

Let's Go!
